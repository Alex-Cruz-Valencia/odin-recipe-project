Red Rice

Description:

The following contains the ingredients needed to make amazing red-rice!

  1. Grate tomatoes into a bowl using a box grater; discard tomato skins
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Add onion; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes
  4. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute
  5. Stir in rice and cook, stirring often, until rice is lightly toasted, about 3 minutes
  6. Stir in grated tomato, chicken broth, and tomato sauce; bring mixture to a boil
  7. Stir in jalape√±o pepper, cilantro, and salt; reduce heat to low
  8. Cover the skillet and simmer until liquid has been absorbed, about 15 minutes
  9. Turn off the heat; let rice stand, covered, for 8 minutes
  10. Fluff with a fork before transferring rice to a serving dish
